Honey Butter Yule Ham Recipe

Home Gingerbread Peppermint Patties Yule Ham


What's better than a juicy, buttery ham to kick off the holidays?

Ahh, Christmas. Loved ones gathered around the fireplace, twinkling lights strung about the home, and the smell of the yuletide ham hot out of the oven. It's the key element to the year-end feast we all prepare, the centerpiece to a beautiful work of art. Add this recipe to your collection and I'm sure you'll never go back to store-bought again.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12lb) bone-in ham
  • 3/4 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup whole cloves (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (175C).
  2. Cut 1in deep criss-crosses into the flat side of the ham. In a small bowl, mix together the butter and honey. Slather onto the ham, making sure to get in the crevices too. Insert cloves into the ham if desired. Place in a roasting pan.
  3. Bake for 4 hours, or 20 minutes per pound. The internal temperature should reach 160F (70C). Baste every 20 minutes with the drippings. Remove, slice, serve, savor.
